PROBLEM SOLVING AND LOGIC SKILLS

At ADA I have been developing the skill of solving problems and thinking logically. I try to remind myself it is not about what I am doing, but HOW I am doing it. It is important to break down the problem and create a habit of asking as many questions as possible to clarify a problem.

I find motivation in my goal of becoming a developer when I find myself using my solving problems skill in my daily tasks. Making decisions was not my strongest skill, I could be stuck in a loop, trying to come up with solutions for simple problems, such as planning my day. Since I started the ADA program my mindset changed and I am able to evaluate my plan, exploring the options and making a decision with confidence and knowing there are lots of ways to solve a problem.
