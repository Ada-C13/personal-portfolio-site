If you are wondering why am I Ada Developers student? Here is your answer:

I am aware that the world has many inequalities and the way in which Ada seeks to give us a space for minorities to improve our lives is simply admirable. I'm fascinated to see how Ada manages to transform people's lives. I have read stories of warriors who have left their home towns and family to be part of Ada. I wanted to be one of them. I wanted to be part of this new way of life!
