Happy Women's History Month! Endless gratitude to the women who paved the path; Elizabeth Blackwell, M.D., Ada Lovelace, Marie Curie, the women who marched on 3.3.1913, Katherine Graham, Katherine Johnson, Grace Hopper, Hillary Clinton, and Michelle Obama. The list is endless. But I'm saying a special thank you to my mom, who stood her ground and powered through as a single parent.
