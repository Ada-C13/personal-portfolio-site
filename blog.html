Online class began

Mar 12, 2020

Covid-19 has finally hit the US. For everyone's health and safety, our classes were moved from onsite to online starting Mar 10, 2020. I won't lie! I enjoyed taking a class in my PJs at first, but I really miss having classes onsite and interacting with my dear friends. At least we have an accountablity group to keep us from feeling too isolated at home during our code journey. This is a scary time for everyone for sure, but I believe that it could be a valuable experience for instructors, staff and students. We can learn something valuable even from this unusual time. For example, we can learn how to create a more robust online education program for future Adies, and build strong support groups virtually (peers to peers, tutors to students, instructors to students). Furthermore, we have a chance to spend more time with our families. Positive mental attitude! That said, I still miss everyone.
