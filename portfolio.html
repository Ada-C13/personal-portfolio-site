OnDelay - Project Manager

OnDelay is a process improvment project, completed as part of Alaska's leadership development course. The assigned project was to provide a recommendation to decrease delays. Our team developened the outline of a program that would track delays in real-time, consolidate flight information, and allow the different workgroups to communicate easier. Resolving delay disputes would decrease from 2 weeks to 48 hours.
