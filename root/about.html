Suely is a problem solver, who values precision, attention to detail and is known to ask tough questions. She has a great desire to learn, constantly measuring her progress, striving to improve herself. Suely takes immense satisfaction in being busy and productive, figuring out what is wrong and addressing it.

She has over 5 years of experience managing a cross-functional team, advising senior management, implementing strategic initiatives, leading projects and keeping them on time and in budget. She is a leader who thrives in motivating her team.

Specific areas of expertise include:

  • Programming experience: Ruby, HTML, CSS, Web APIs, Minitest, TDD, and CS Fundamentals
  • Excellent communication skills, presentation to internal and external audiences
  • Data analysis and visualization to communicate insights
  • Leadership and organizational skills, including cross-group relationship management
  • International experience and communication in English, Portuguese, and Spanish
  • Project, risk, and budget management

Interests

Suely is originally from Fortaleza, Brazil and now lives in Redmond, Washington. When she is not coding or solving puzzles, she enjoys walking on trails, and listening to music. Her favorite bands are Coldplay, U2, Imagine Dragons, OneRepublic, and Mumford & Sons.

Suely loves Brazilian coffee and used to harvest Arabica beans in the Maciço do Baturité, Ceará, to brew her own coffee. She didn’t use any machines in the process, just her hands, to obtain high quality beans with minimum environmental impact.
