Hi! I'm Stephanie

I worked in the airline industry for 13 years, my experience as a Loadmaster in Operations also includes Project Management, Training Development, and Classroom Instruction.

I am currently learning software development at Ada Developers Academy

I am a California native and enjoy all things sunshine and water. I am excited to represent Native American Women in tech.

I have a senior beagle named June, which has led me to living a cruelty free lifestyle. Did you know, unless you have made conscious decisions not to, your kitchen and bathroom are probably full of products tested on animals? Beagles are used for animal testing because of their docile and forgiving nature.

I consider myself a creative person, I love all things art and design. I am hoping to bring my creative side to wherever I end up in the tech world!

Here are some causes I care about!

  • Beagle Freedom Project - Organization that fights for cruelty-free legistature, and rescues animals that have been used in experimental research.
  • Coalition to Stop Violence Against Native Women - Organization working to stop violence against Native women and children by advocating for social change.
  • Adaptive Surf League - Promotes Adaptive Surfing as a professional sport for differently abled surfers.
