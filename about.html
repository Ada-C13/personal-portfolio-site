About

My name is Jessica Liang, and I am a growing developer at Ada Developer's Academy. Prior to transitioning to computer programming, I worked in the healthcare field where I encountered many technologies that could be updated to be more efficient and capable. My working experiences ignited a desire to solve these issues and sparked an interest in software development. I look forward seeing my progression and how I grow.
