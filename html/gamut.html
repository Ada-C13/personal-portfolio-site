Gamut: The Millenial Mag

Gamut is a concept magazine I developed. Designed to cater to both female and male-identifying millennials, Gamut aims to bridge the gendered communication gaps between men’s and women’s print and digital publications. My colleague, Gabrielle Wilkosz, and I provided the written content for the magazine. The design and layout was conceived and executed by me.

Specifications

  • Must provide a unique, non-gendered reading experience that touches on a variety of human interests.

Made with Unsplash, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe InDesign
