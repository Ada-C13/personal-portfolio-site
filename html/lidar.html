Lidar Infographic

Do you know what LIDAR is? LIDAR stands for Light Detection and Ranging, and it's used to gather geographic information. While the system itself is relatively simple, I found there was a lack of novice-friendly information about it, so I created this infographic "on spec" to help others dip their toes into the LIDAR realm.
